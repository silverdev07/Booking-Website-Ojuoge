Before You Book

Booking Policies

Please read through our policies carefully. By booking with us, you agree to these terms.

💳

Deposit Requirement

A minimum deposit of 60% is required to secure your appointment date. This deposit is collected in advance via bank transfer before your session is confirmed.

💰

Balance Payment

The remaining balance must be paid on or before your service is completed. Please come prepared with the outstanding amount on the day of your appointment.

🗓️

Cancellations & Rescheduling

Need to reschedule? Your deposit stays valid and will be transferred to your new date. However, you must inform us at least 24 hours before your appointment.

⏱️

Late Arrival

We offer a 30-minute grace period for late arrivals. After 30 minutes, a late fee of ₦10,000 per hour will be charged until your session begins.

🏠

Studio Guidelines

Children are not permitted in the studio. To ensure every client enjoys a calm, comfortable experience, please bring no more than one guest to accompany you. We have a small, intimate space and we prioritise our clients' comfort.
